About Us

Gutz Root is a unique fusion of greenery and craft cocktails. Step into a warm, lush environment filled with houseplants, rare species, and towering trees — all surrounding a full bar set in the heart of the shop. Whether you're here for a new fernfriend or a botanically-inspired drink, Gutz Root blends nature and community into one unforgettable experience.

Our Plants

From tropical foliage to hardy desert succulents, our plant collection grows with the seasons. Every plant is hand-selected for health, beauty, and personality — just waiting for its new home.

The Bar at Gutz Root

Our full bar features botanical cocktails, herb-infused syrups, small-batch spirits, and zero-proof options inspired by the natural world. Sip among the leaves in a space designed to bring people together.
