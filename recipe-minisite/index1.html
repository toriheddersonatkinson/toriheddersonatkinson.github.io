Shepherd's Pie

Comforting Irish shepherd's pie with seasoned lamb, vegetables and a golden mashed potato topping.

  • Serves: 4
  • Prep: 25 min
  • Cook: 45 min

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Potatoes: Boil potatoes until tender (≈20 min). Mash with butter, milk, salt and pepper.
  3. Filling: Sauté onion & carrots in oil until soft. Add ground lamb, brown. Stir in garlic, thyme and flour, then add tomato paste, Worcestershire, stock and peas. Simmer until thickened.
  4. Transfer filling to an ovenproof dish, spread mashed potatoes on top and create peaks for browning.
  5. Bake 15–20 minutes until top is golden. Rest 10 minutes and serve.

Ingredients

Filling

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 cup onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1½ lb ground lamb
  • 2 garlic cloves, grated
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 3 tsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup beef stock

Potato Topping

  • 2 lb potatoes, peeled & chopped
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • ¼ cup milk
  • Salt & pepper to taste
Tip: pipe the potato for a crisp, decorative top.
