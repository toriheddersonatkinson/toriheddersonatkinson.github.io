White Bean Chicken Chili

~45 min • Dinner • Hearty

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breasts, shredded
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cans white beans, drained
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • spices, lime, toppings

Steps

  1. Sear and shred chicken; set aside.
  2. Sauté aromatics, add beans and broth; simmer.
  3. Add chicken back and simmer. Finish with lime and toppings.
