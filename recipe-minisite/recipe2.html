Lemon Salmon

~20 min • Dinner • Seafood

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon (zest + juice)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C) or heat a skillet.
  2. Mix oil, lemon, garlic, salt and pepper; brush on salmon.
  3. Bake 10–12 minutes or sear 3–4 minutes per side. Garnish and serve.
