Lemon Salmon

Easy oven-baked lemon salmon with herbs and crispy edges.

  • Serves: 2–4
  • Prep: 10 min
  • Cook: 15–20 min

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place salmon on a baking sheet, brush with olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper. Add herbs.
  3. Bake for 12–18 minutes depending on thickness. Serve with lemon wedges.

Ingredients

  • 1–1.5 lb salmon fillet
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced + wedges
  • 1 tsp dried dill or fresh parsley
  • Salt & pepper
Tip: oven times depend on fillet thickness — check at 12 minutes.
