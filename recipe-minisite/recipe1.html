Shepherd's Pie

About 1 hour • Dinner • Comfort

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground lamb or beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup stock
  • 3 cups mashed potatoes
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Sauté vegetables, brown meat, stir in tomato paste and stock.
  3. Transfer to dish, top with mashed potatoes, dot with butter.
  4. Bake 20–25 minutes until golden. Rest 5 minutes and serve.
