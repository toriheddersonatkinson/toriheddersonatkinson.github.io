White Bean Chicken Chili

Hearty, comforting chili made with shredded chicken, white beans, green chiles and warm spices.

  • Serves: 4–6
  • Prep: 15 min
  • Cook: 35–40 min

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large pot over medium heat. Sauté 1 medium chopped onion until translucent (about 5 minutes).
  2. Add 2 minced garlic cloves and 1 tsp ground cumin and 1 tsp dried oregano; cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add 2 cans (15 oz each) drained and rinsed white beans, 1 can (4–4.5 oz) chopped green chiles, 3 cups low-sodium chicken broth, and 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts (or thighs).
  4. Bring to a simmer, reduce heat to low, cover and cook 20–25 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Remove chicken, shred with two forks, then return to pot. Stir in 1/2 tsp salt (adjust to taste) and 1/4 tsp black pepper. Simmer 5 more minutes to meld flavors.
  6. Finish with juice of 1 lime and chopped fresh cilantro to taste. Serve with optional toppings: shredded cheese, avocado, sour cream, or tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 (15 oz) cans white beans (great northern or cannellini), drained & rinsed
  • 1 (4–4.5 oz) can chopped green chiles
  • 2–3 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts (or thighs)
  • Salt & black pepper, to taste
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)
Tip: For a quicker version, use 3 cups cooked shredded rotisserie chicken and reduce simmer time; adjust broth so chili reaches desired thickness.
