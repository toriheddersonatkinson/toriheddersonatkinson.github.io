Pacific Rhododendron

Alternate Names

Also known as Rhododendron macrophyllum, this plant is commonly referred to as the Coast Rhododendron.

Physical Size

The Pacific Rhododendron can grow up to 15 feet tall and 10 feet wide, with large, leathery leaves that can reach up to 12 inches in length.

Geography

This species is native to the Pacific Northwest region of North America, thriving in coastal forests and mountainous areas.

Climate

The Pacific Rhododendron prefers a mild, moist climate with well-drained soil and partial shade.
